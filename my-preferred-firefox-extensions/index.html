My preferred firefox extensions

Jonathan Ródenas López

I use a few extensions to improve my daily browsing experience.

The complete list:

  • 600% Volume
  • ClearURLs
  • Decentraleyes
  • LanguageTool
  • linkding Extension (only if you have a self-Managed linkding instance)
  • NopeCHA
  • Privacy Badger
  • SponsorBlock
  • SurfingKeys
  • TranquilityReader
  • uBlock Origin
  • FastForward
  • Youtube NonStop
